# English on UCNS — corrected research boundary

## Status

This document is a downstream research specification. It is not public-gonol
canon, not a proof that English semantics are geometric, and not a theorem-status
bridge into EDCM or edcmbone.

The previous version flattened three separate surfaces:

1. the canonical public gonol;
2. normalized recursive factorization objects;
3. EDCM/edcmbone token and measurement encodings.

It also described bounded composition probes as full-depth proof and treated an
internal carrier projection as the dimensionality of language. Those claims are
superseded.

---

## 0. Authority and source order

### UCNS public frame

The exact public gonol from
`The-Interdependency/a0-betatest@7af8debf6ef3905f01baff02b43d8c3bee16ccbc`
is canon for all UCNS.

```text
position 0 = SPACE = ZERO
position 0 = Möbius twist point = seam = fixed system origin
one 360-degree circuit changes orientation

```

Its arrangement, faces, chirality, origin-fixed mirror, private-transform rule,
and lifted traversal are authoritative.

### UCNS normalized factorization model

`UCNSObject`, multiplication, quotient, catalogue search, and `n_min` form an
internal algebra. No public-gonol-to-factorization bridge is assumed.

### EDCM / edcmbone

Token vocabularies, grammatical features, metric axes, transcripts, and readouts
are downstream empirical or encoding surfaces. They do not own the public gonol
and do not inherit UCNS proof status.

## 1. What “freight” may mean

English may be carried by UCNS only through an explicit, versioned adapter.
The adapter must declare:

- source text and normalization policy;
- public-gonol characters or lifted paths used;
- treatment of SPACE seam events;
- treatment of unsupported characters;
- whether any normalized factorization object is constructed;
- the exact bridge used for that construction;
- information loss and recoverability;
- authority and proof-status non-transfer.

Without such a bridge, these are separate records:

```text
public-gonol text path
linguistic token/morphology evidence
normalized factorization object
EDCM measurement output
```

They must not be silently identified.

## 2. Layer 0 — public-gonol text traversal

The only canonical character-level UCNS surface currently available is the
public-gonol lifted traversal.

A valid Layer-0 implementation may:

- encode carrier characters through the exact public arrangement;
- preserve absolute lifted order;
- emit SPACE as the seam;
- preserve full-revolution repeated characters;
- decode exactly over the carrier alphabet;
- attach external linguistic metadata separately.

It may not:

- replace carrier positions with hash-derived locations;
- convert positions into unratified fractions or angles;
- case-fold or delete spaces implicitly;
- infer grammatical class or meaning from carrier position alone;
- treat 360 degrees as complete return.

## 3. Closed-token and grammatical encodings

Historical EDCM/edcmbone closed-token objects use local host lattices and feature
payloads. They are application encodings, not the public gonol and not automatic
UCNS canon.

Their current status is:

```text
implementation evidence: repository-specific
public-gonol bridge: absent unless explicitly supplied
linguistic validity: empirical / research question
UCNS theorem transfer: false
```

A 16-gonal grammatical class table or 32-gonal feature object may be retained as
an EDCM encoding experiment, but it must not be described as the universal UCNS
carrier or as derived from the public twist origin without a proved bridge.

The internal factorization unit is not public SPACE/ZERO. Avoiding collapse to an
internal unit is an application-encoding concern, not a definition of the system
origin.

## 4. Sequence composition

A sequence may be represented in at least two distinct ways:

### 4.1 Public lifted path

Order is preserved by absolute traversal positions relative to the fixed seam.
This path is lossless over the carrier alphabet.

### 4.2 Internal factorization product

A sequence of normalized factorization objects may be multiplied using the
existing internal operation.

No current theorem proves that these two sequence constructions are equivalent.
A bridge must establish:

- element mapping;
- order preservation;
- origin/twist preservation;
- orientation after 360 and 720 degrees;
- face/chirality treatment;
- composition correspondence;
- recoverability.

Until then, multiplying token objects is a factorization-model experiment, not a
public-gonol language theorem.

## 5. Carrier and dimensionality claims

The public carrier is the exact 157-position twist-bearing gonol.

The internal `n_min` is a projected denominator invariant of normalized
factorization objects. Carrier-LCM is proved only for that definition on its
declared domain.

Therefore these claims are not presently justified:

- sentence dimensionality equals the LCM of word carriers in the complete UCNS
  system;
- prime factors of internal `n_min` are semantic field dimensions;
- vocabulary automatically generates a hyperdimensional linguistic space;
- public-gonol completeness follows from Carrier-LCM.

Those may remain research hypotheses if explicitly labeled and tested.

## 6. Field embeddings

A field embedding

```text
Phi : source representation -> vector / geometry representation
```

must declare its source representation. It cannot use the generic word “UCNS
object” while switching among public paths, normalized factorization objects,
and EDCM records.

A candidate embedding must provide:

1. schema and version;
2. exact input domain;
3. exact output codomain;
4. deterministic construction;
5. composition law, if claimed;
6. negative/counterexample tests;
7. information-loss statement;
8. corpus-scale empirical evaluation;
9. proof-status and measurement-status boundaries.

A bounded probe showing equality on selected fixtures is `TEST-BACKED` for those
fixtures. It is not “proven at full depth” unless a proof covers the stated
recursive domain and all transitive obligations.

---

## 7. Chirality, pairing, and reflection

The public gonol supplies canonical face, chirality, adjacency, and origin-fixed
mirror behavior.

A linguistic claim such as “opening and closing punctuation are the same object
under reflection” requires an explicit encoding and proof/test of that encoding.
It does not follow merely because both glyphs appear on mirrored positions of the
public arrangement or because an application uses an internal disk flip.

Likewise, irony, sarcasm, grammatical polarity, and semantic mediation are not
identified by face XOR without independent semantic evidence.

---

## 8. Morphology

Roots, affixes, words, clauses, and utterances may be modeled as separate grains,
but the public gonol does not assign their semantic composition automatically.

A morphology adapter must distinguish:

```text
glyph traversal
surface rendering
morphological evidence
semantic authority
factorization object
measurement readout
```

All valid parses may be retained as alternatives. Selection or superposition
requires a declared policy. No parse becomes true because it can be encoded.

---

## 9. Success criteria

A defensible English/UCNS result requires separate evidence for:

- lossless public-gonol text encoding;
- correctness of any morphology parser;
- correctness of any public-to-factorization bridge;
- composition correspondence;
- metric definition;
- empirical linguistic usefulness;
- bounded memory and reproducibility at corpus scale;
- non-transfer of proof and measurement status.

The strongest current canonical result is the public-gonol traversal itself.
Everything beyond it must name its adapter and evidence.

---

## 10. Forbidden claims

Do not state, without new evidence, that:

- English semantics are intrinsic public-gonol geometry;
- token class is inherently its public carrier address;
- sentence meaning is the internal factorization product;
- internal `n_min` is linguistic dimensionality;
- a short probe proves full-depth field composition;
- public face/chirality encodes sarcasm or irony;
- quotient operations make linguistic encoding bijective;
- Theorem N validates a language embedding;
- EDCM metrics are UCNS factorization outputs.
